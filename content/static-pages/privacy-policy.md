# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026

WristNerd ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website wristnerd.xyz (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our Site.

By using our Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Voluntarily

- **Email address** — when you subscribe to our newsletter, use the Gift Finder Quiz email capture, or contact us through our contact form
- **Name** — if provided when contacting us
- **Message content** — when you submit a contact form inquiry

### Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

- **Log data** — IP address, browser type, browser version, pages visited, time and date of visit, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data
- **Cookies and tracking technologies** — small data files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience (see "Cookies" section below)
- **Device information** — device type, operating system, unique device identifiers

### Information from Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information about you:

- **Google Analytics (GA4)** — website traffic analysis and user behavior insights
- **Email service provider (ConvertKit/Mailchimp)** — email subscription management and campaign delivery
- **Affiliate networks (Amazon Associates, ShareASale, CJ Affiliate)** — conversion tracking via cookies placed when you click affiliate links

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **To provide and maintain the Site** — ensuring proper functionality and user experience
- **To send email newsletters** — if you've subscribed, we'll send watch recommendations, deals, and guides (you can unsubscribe at any time)
- **To analyze Site usage** — understanding how visitors interact with our content helps us improve guides and recommendations
- **To respond to inquiries** — replying to contact form submissions and emails
- **To track affiliate conversions** — understanding which product recommendations lead to purchases so we can improve our guides
- **To comply with legal obligations** — responding to lawful requests from authorities

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Use your data for targeted advertising on other platforms
- Share your email address with other companies for marketing purposes

## Cookies

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you interact with content.

### Cookies We Use

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Provider | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|----------|
| **Essential** | Site functionality (e.g., dismissing announcement bar) | WristNerd | Session / 30 days |
| **Analytics** | Understanding traffic and user behavior | Google Analytics | Up to 2 years |
| **Affiliate** | Tracking product clicks for commission attribution | Amazon Associates, ShareASale, CJ | 24 hours–90 days (varies) |
| **Email** | Remembering subscription status | ConvertKit/Mailchimp | 1 year |

### Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings > Privacy > Cookies

Note: Disabling cookies may affect certain Site features, such as the announcement bar dismiss function.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information, including:

- SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS) on all pages

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### For EU/UK Residents (GDPR)
- Right to access your personal data
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

### For California Residents (CCPA)
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to request deletion of personal information
- Right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

### For All Users
- Right to unsubscribe from email communications at any time
- Right to request a copy of your personal data
- Right to request deletion of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **hello@wristnerd.xyz**.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom (UK), we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

| Purpose | Legal Basis |
|---------|-------------|
