## FAQ

### What's the best Christmas watch gift for a husband?

The **Tissot PRX** ($325–$375) is our top pick for husbands — the integrated steel bracelet looks luxurious, the sapphire crystal won't scratch, and it's Swiss-made. If you want to go bigger, the **Tissot Gentleman Powermatic 80** ($600–$700) is a truly special gift. For a more affordable option, the **Seiko Presage SRPD37** ($280–$320) is stunning.

### What's a good Christmas watch gift under $100?

The **Casio G-Shock GA2100** ($80–$110) is our favorite stocking stuffer — indestructible, sleek, and affordable. The **Citizen Eco-Drive BM8180** ($75–$100) is the better choice if he prefers a classic look over sporty.

### Should I buy a watch online or in-store for Christmas?

Online gives you better prices, more selection, and easier returns (especially Amazon). In-store lets you see the watch in person and avoid shipping delays. Our recommendation: browse our guide online, decide on the watch, and buy from wherever has the best deal and delivery timeline.

### What if he doesn't like the watch?

Amazon has a generous return policy, and most watches purchased in November–December have extended holiday return windows through January. Keep the receipt, don't remove tags until Christmas, and if in doubt, include a gift receipt.

### Is it okay to buy a watch for a new boyfriend for Christmas?

Absolutely — just match the price to the stage of the relationship. Under a year? Stay under $200 (Orient Bambino, Seiko 5, Fossil Neutra). Over a year? You can go higher (Tissot PRX, Seiko Presage). The watch is thoughtful regardless of price.

### When do Christmas watch deals start?

Black Friday (late November) is the biggest watch sale period. Many watches on this list drop 15–30% during Black Friday and Cyber Monday. Amazon also runs deals throughout December. We'll update this guide with deal alerts as they happen.
